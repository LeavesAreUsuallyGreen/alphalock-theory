
# Frequently Asked Questions About Alphalock

## What is Alphalock?

Alphalock is a constrained-feedback deductive word game and Wordle alternative designed to explore reasoning under uncertainty, information gain, and entropy-based decision making.

Unlike Wordle, Alphalock does not reveal which letters are in the correct positions. Instead, players receive only aggregate counts of:

- correct letters
- misplaced letters
- incorrect letters

This changes the reasoning process and increases ambiguity.

---

## Is Alphalock a Wordle alternative?

Yes.

Players sometimes compare Alphalock to **Wordle** because both involve solving hidden words through feedback across multiple guesses.

However, Alphalock changes the feedback system significantly by removing positional information.

As a result, strategies that work well in Wordle may not transfer directly.

---

## Is Alphalock harder than Wordle?

Difficulty depends on player preference.

Many players may find Alphalock harder because:

- less information is revealed after each guess
- uncertainty remains longer
- multiple possible solutions often survive deeper into the game

Players who enjoy:

- logic puzzles
- deduction
- reasoning under uncertainty

may prefer Alphalock despite the increased ambiguity.

---

## How is Alphalock different from Wordle?

Wordle provides:

✓ exact positional clues

✓ direct elimination opportunities

Alphalock provides:

✓ counts of correct and misplaced letters

✗ no positional feedback

The removal of positional information changes how players form and test hypotheses.

## Is Alphalock similar to Mastermind?

In some ways, yes.

Although Alphalock is a word game, its constrained feedback can make it conceptually closer to **Mastermind** than traditional Wordle.

Both emphasize:

- deduction
- elimination
- uncertainty reduction
- reasoning from incomplete information
