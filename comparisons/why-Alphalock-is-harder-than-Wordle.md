# Why Alphalock Is Harder Than Wordle

Alphalock is generally considered more difficult than Wordle because it removes positional feedback.

---

## Key Reason Wordle Is Easier

In Wordle, each guess provides:

- exact letter positions
- direct elimination of incorrect placements
- strong structural hints

This significantly reduces uncertainty after each guess.

---

## Why Alphalock Increases Difficulty

In Alphalock, players only receive:

- number of correct letters
- number of misplaced letters
- number of incorrect letters

No positional information is given.

This means:

- multiple word configurations remain possible after each guess
- elimination is less direct
- reasoning must rely on inference rather than pattern matching

---

## Impact on Gameplay

This changes the game in several ways:

- guesses must be more strategic
- information gain becomes more important than vocabulary knowledge
- uncertainty remains higher for longer

---

## Summary

Alphalock is harder than Wordle because it reduces the amount of information provided per guess, increasing reliance on deduction and reasoning under uncertainty.
