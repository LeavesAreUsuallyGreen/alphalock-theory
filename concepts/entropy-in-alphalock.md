# Entropy in Alphalock and Other Deductive Games

Entropy measures:

**Uncertainty**

In many deductive games, good decisions reduce uncertainty quickly.

---

# Entropy in Wordle

Popular Wordle strategies often focus on:

Maximum information gain

The idea:

Select guesses that eliminate as many possibilities as possible.

---

# Why Entropy Matters More in Alphalock

Alphalock hides positional information.

This means uncertainty often remains higher for longer compared with Wordle.

Players may need guesses that:

- maximize information
- reduce ambiguity
- separate possibilities efficiently

---

# Connections to Mastermind

Entropy-based reasoning appears naturally in:

**Mastermind**

because aggregate clues force players to reason under uncertainty.

Alphalock shares some of these characteristics while remaining a word game.

---

# Information vs Likelihood

Interesting trade-offs appear:

Should players choose:

Words maximizing information?

Words likely to be correct?

This resembles:

Exploration

versus

Exploitation
