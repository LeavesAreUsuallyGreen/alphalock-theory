# What Makes Alphalock Different?

Alphalock introduces:

**Constrained feedback**

Players receive incomplete information and must:

- infer possibilities
- update beliefs
- revise strategies

This changes the reasoning process.

# Why Compare Alphalock to Wordle?

Wordle is widely recognized.

Alphalock changes one important assumption:

Removing positional feedback.

This creates different reasoning demands and potentially different solving strategies.
